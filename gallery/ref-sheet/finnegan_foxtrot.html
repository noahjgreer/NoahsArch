Finnegan Foxtrot

Finni is a small Christian fennec foxcat. He is well known for his love of cozy things, such as hot cocoa, rainstorms, reading the Bible, and cuddling with his friends. Finnegan's favorite attire consists of comfy, baggy knit sweaters, short shorts of some kind, and thigh highs and/or arm warmers. Finni typically sports a bandana and never goes anywhere without his kitty collar, which features a little bell.

Finni is known for having a chibi, kemono, cutesy bodily structure overall, with an androgynous figure. Finni has exceedingly feminine traits (larger hip structure), but is not female. He is pretty short, and the volume of his tail is roughly equivalent to his body, measuring 3/4 of his height, and about the same diameter as his width. Finni is also notable for his big fennec ears and short snout, courtesy of his cat genetics. Finni's facial structure most closely resembles a cat, being the only part of his body that is most catlike.
