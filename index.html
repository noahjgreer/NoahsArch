About Me

My name is Noah Greer, and I am a 15 year old with a passion for creating. I like to draw, create websites, make videos, code, along with a slew of other hobbies that involve creativity. I also have an extreme love for foxes and other furry things. In my spare time, I enjoy diving into God's Word and dissecting and studying the Bible while growing closer to Him. My goal in creating Noah's Arch is to bring together all of my creations into an archive where they can be organized and kept safe forever.
